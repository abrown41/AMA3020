\section{\href{https://doi.org/10.1007/s10884-017-9599-x}{ Rapidly and Slowly Oscillating Periodic Solutions of a Delayed Van der Pol Oscillator}}
 \subsection*{Supervisor: Dr Gabor Kiss}


In this paper, we introduce a method to prove existence of several rapidly and slowly oscillating periodic solutions of a delayed Van der Pol oscillator. The proof is a combination of pen and paper analytic estimates, the contraction mapping theorem and a computer program using interval arithmetic.
